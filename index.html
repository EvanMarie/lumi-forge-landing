The Lumi Forge Creator Library

Flexibility to Create

As a creator on Lumi Forge, you have multiple powerful paths to bring your vision to life:

Self-Service, Full-Service, and Hybrid

  • The Creator Studio (Self-Service): Use our suite of tools to build and maintain your spaces yourself. Write manually in our Content Forge editor, or leverage our AI to transform your ideas into polished Resources, Explorations, and Luminations.
  • Full-Service Transformation: Let our development team handle everything. Simply describe how you want explorers to engage with your work, and we'll bring that vision to life using Lumi Forge's proprietary framework.
  • Hybrid Approach: Combine our tools with our development team's expertise. Our methods are as unique as our creators.

AI-Powered Creation Tools

Lumi Forge harnesses AI to accelerate your creation process. Whether you have a polished PDF ready to transform or just a collection of ideas, our tools meet you where you are.

  • Upload & Transform PDFs: Upload your PDF documents and transform them into rich, interactive Explorations. Our system parses your content and helps you extract key concepts, structure chapters, rewrite dense text into accessible language, and generate interactive questions.
  • Choose Your Format: Select from multiple layout styles including Standard Article, Step-by-Step Guide, Case Study, Academic Paper, and more. The AI structures your content to match your selection.
  • Customize Tone & Voice: Maintain your original wording or adjust the style to be Professional, Casual, Enthusiastic, Socratic, or Concise. This ensures your content resonates with your specific audience.
  • Interactive Components: Include specialty elements like 3D Image Carousels, Highlight Panels, Interactive Timelines, or Check Your Understanding questions directly in your generated content.
  • Seamless Editing: Generate a draft structure, apply your chosen tone, then immediately jump into manual editing to tweak, refine, or rewrite sections. AI handles the heavy lifting while you maintain creative control.

Your Rights & Protections

Your content belongs to you. Whether we build your interactive experience for you or you create it yourself using the Creator Studio, your intellectual property remains yours.

  • Complete IP Ownership: Your manuscripts, videos, prompts, and all interactive experiences we create remain your property. We don't grant any licenses to AI providers to use your work.
  • Privacy-First AI: We use strict privacy settings and enterprise-grade AI services with commercial terms that strictly prohibit training on your data. Your content is never used to train AI models.
  • Zero Data Retention: When you use Creator Studio, data sent to AI providers is ephemeral—used only to generate your response and then discarded. Your content stays confidential.
  • No Third-Party Training: We do not consent to your content being used to train any third-party models. Your intellectual property is protected at every step.
